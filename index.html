Meet the Designer: Stacy Reynolds

About Me

Hello! My name is Stacy Reynolds. By day, I work as an accountant, but I have always had a creative side that I love exploring. I am new to coding and graphic design, but I;m excitrd to be learning and building my skills every day. Right now I'm focused on HTML, CSS, and design basics, and this portfolio is where I am sharing the projects I've created along the way

What I enjoy most about design and coding is the change to mix creativity with problem solving. Even as a beginner, I've found that every project teaches me something new and gives me the motivation to keep going.

Outside of learning and creating, I love hiking and making homwmade perfumes and bath scrubs. These hobbies, much like coding and design, let me explore creativity in different ways.

I'm looking forward to growing my skills, taking on new challenges, and continuing this journey into the world of design.
